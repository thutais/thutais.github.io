TAIS is a vibrant community founded in 2019 by a group of AI enthusiasts from Tsinghua University.

### Our Mission

To explore the potential of human and artificial intelligence through collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

### Events and Activities

TAIS has successfully organized various events, including:

- Talks.
- Mini conferences.
- AI + Blockchain events.
- Get Started with AI workshops.
- TAIS AI Talk Series.
- Weekly seminars on AI and new technology topics.

### Competitions

Our team actively participates in hackathons and competitions, including:

- Numerai Tournament
- AI SDG - Disaster Reduction Competition
- AWS Disaster Response Hackathon (Best Solution Award)

### Collaborations

**Current Collaborators:**

- **Industry:** [SRII](https://thesrii.org) (Service Research and Innovation Institute in California)
- **Academic:**
  - Tsinghua University Student Association of Big Data Research
  - Tsinghua University School of Software
  - Robotics Club, Tsinghua University
  - [I2 Labs from University of Washington](https://i2-labs.org) (focus on AI in neuroscience)

**Potential Future Collaborators:**

- Sugon Smart Computing Information Technology Co. Ltd
- Junlingsihai (Beijing Junling Sihai Technology Co. Ltd)

### Join Us

TAIS invites you to join us in advancing artificial intelligence and solving real-world problems through innovation and partnership. Discover the future of AI with us.
